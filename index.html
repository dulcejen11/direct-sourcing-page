The shift

Direct Sourcing isn't a static talent database. It's an operating model that gets stronger every time you use it.

  • Reconnect with talent you already know and trust.
  • Activate proven workers in days, not weeks.
  • Build a ready-to-deploy bench of vetted, engaged people.
The engine

Three moves, run as a loop

Each pass makes the next hire faster, cheaper, and a better match than the one before it.

Activate

Keep proven talent ready

Hold onto the contractors who already delivered, ready for redeployment instead of lost back to agencies.

  • Lower recurring agency spend
  • Faster access to trusted talent
  • Fewer redundant sourcing cycles
Engage

Make the next hire smarter

Every engagement adds data and trust, so hiring gets faster and more predictable as the community grows.

  • Shorter, more predictable time-to-fill
  • Higher performance and match quality
  • Less dependency on external sourcing
Deploy

Bring talent back

Turn contingent workers into an engaged community that chooses you again instead of the open market.

  • Higher redeployment and retention
  • Stronger employer brand with contractors
  • More talent available, less competition
